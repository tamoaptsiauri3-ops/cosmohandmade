# Roadmap

## Goal

Build an open-source digital toolkit that helps small craft, fashion, accessories and creative manufacturing businesses use AI and structured data to become more organized, export-ready and transparent.

The toolkit starts from COSMO's real business context: handmade production, direct retail, personalization, repair, Open Workshop experiences, corporate gifts and circular waste-to-product experimentation.

## Phase 1: Foundation - June and July 2026

Expected outputs:

- Public repository with README, MIT License and project links;
- Handmade product data schema;
- Example product records with material, dimension, care, personalization and circularity fields;
- Georgian-English product storytelling prompt;
- Evaluation checklist for product copy, buyer readiness and non-greenwashing language;
- OpenAI credit use plan.

## Phase 2: Content and Catalogue Workflows - July and August 2026

Expected outputs:

- Product page generation workflow for WooCommerce and marketplaces;
- B2B line-sheet template for concept stores, museum shops and lifestyle boutiques;
- SEO metadata workflow for Georgian and English product pages;
- Buyer-ready product story blocks connected to materials, craft and personalization;
- Simple examples that non-technical SME teams can adapt.

## Phase 3: Personalization and Gift Builder Prototype - August and September 2026

Expected outputs:

- Data model for engraving, initials, color choices, packaging and gift messages;
- Prototype assistant flow for personal and corporate gift selection;
- Export-ready brief format for internal production teams;
- Test cases for avoiding missing personalization details.

## Phase 4: Circular Waste-to-Product Documentation - September and October 2026

Expected outputs:

- Offcut tracking template for leather and textile waste;
- Documentation model for converting production waste into functional design objects;
- Before/after product documentation format;
- Responsible language checklist to avoid vague sustainability claims.

## Phase 5: Public Demo and SME Feedback - October and November 2026

Expected outputs:

- Small web demo or documented workflow;
- Feedback from COSMO team members and selected creative SMEs;
- Updated schema and prompt examples;
- Practical guide for adapting the toolkit outside COSMO.

## Success metrics

- 20 structured product records prepared;
- 10 Georgian-English product descriptions generated and reviewed;
- 3 B2B-ready product line-sheet examples prepared;
- 1 personalization/gift-builder workflow prototyped;
- 1 circular waste-to-product documentation workflow prepared;
- clear README and documentation usable by another small creative business.
